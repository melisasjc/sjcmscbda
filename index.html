WELCOME TO MSC DATA ANALYTICS
VISION

The Department of Big Data Analytics, established in the year 2016-17, is committed to training students to analyse Big Data and to arrive at valuable insights in the domain. In this way the department hopes to graduate qualified professionals with a secular, progressive and socially responsible outlook who will play a pivotal role in meeting the demands of the rapidly expanding digital economy.

MISSION

In a world dominated by digital technology, with voluminous data being generated there is a dire need for professionals capable of analyzing and managing big data and this department is committed to the task of producing them, charting out a course. The program of study hopes to achieve this through active theoretical and practical engagement with cutting edge knowledge in the field while at the same time pursuing human excellence and service to society through outreach programs.
